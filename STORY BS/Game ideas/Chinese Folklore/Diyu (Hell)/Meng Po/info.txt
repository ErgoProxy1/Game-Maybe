The Old Lady Meng Po, Lady of Forgetfulness of the 10th Court of Diyu.
It is her job to ensure that souls who are ready to leave Diyu forget their mortal lives before reincarnation.
They also forget their time in Diyu.
She awaits them at Fengdu, the 9th Court of Diyu (Further Research).
Collects Herbs around ponds to make the Five Flavored Tea of Forgetfulness, permanent amnesia.
